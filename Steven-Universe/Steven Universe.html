Steven Universe is a cartoon created by Rebecca Sugar for the Cartoon Network. It's about the misadventures of young Steven Universe, a young Gem/Human hybrid, and his surrogate family of gems, known as the Crystal Gems.

These Gems, Pearl, Amethyst, and Garnet, fight to protect the Earth from the Homeworld Gems, whose attempts to harvest the planet's resources for themselves were thwarted millinia ago by Steven's mother Rose Quartz.

Rose eventually met Steven's dad, Greg Universe, and the two fell in love. She gave up her physical form in order to give birth to Steven, who inherited her gem as well as her abilities. Now it's up to him to help the Crystal Gems keep Earth and the citizens of Beach City safe from the machinations of Homeworld, who have a renewed interest in the planet, and the corrupted Gems that run amock.
